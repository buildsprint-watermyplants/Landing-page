What we offer

Organize.

Keep track of all your plants in one place, right from the dashboard.

Record.

Quickly record watering so you can get back to your other tasks.

Our Mission

In a time when there's an app for organizing just about every aspect of our lives, this isn't easily available for plant care.


Water my Plants aims to help you get more organized with your plant care by keeping track of your plants. We want to help you become a savvy plant parent and never again have to remember when you watered last.
